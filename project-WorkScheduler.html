Work Scheduler

A Solution that provide you with the tools to Schedule your online meeting , with a custom Video Conference Solution .

Live Link

Project Overview

Scheduling is the art of planning your activities to achieve your goals and priorities in the available time . When done effectively, it helps you: Have enough time for family and friends, for exercise, and for your hobbies. and to acheive this you will need a meeting scheduling app,like WorkScheduler.

Work Scheduler makes the management and scheduling of your online meeting much easier, offer an integrated video conference solution, It's generate meeting link automatically once you schedule a meeting ,and more....
